Our Solution: Prototype Steps

  1. Step 1: Material Selection

    We chose biodegradable and recycled materials to minimize environmental impact.

  2. Step 2: Prototype Design

    Design focused on durability while maintaining eco-friendliness and ease of recyclability.

  3. Step 3: Manufacturing Process

    Implemented low-energy manufacturing methods that reduce waste output.

  4. Step 4: Testing & Feedback

    Rigorous tests on durability and environmental impact were performed with great results.
